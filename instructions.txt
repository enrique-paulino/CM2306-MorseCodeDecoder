After PI is connected to monitor, open node red console.
Login to the thingsboard account > Open "RaspPiDashboard"
Make all the inputs you want > tap the button twice to send the data to things board.
(If no changes happened on thingsboard refresh the page and the most recent message should be there).

If you click at:
  1 BEEP = DOT .
  3 BEEP = DASH -
  7 BEEP = SPACE (between words)
  
  ONE TAP = SPACE (between letters)
  TWO TAP = SEND MESSAGE (TAP 2 times again to reset message)
